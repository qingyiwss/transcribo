# Transcribo — Product Hunt Launch Kit

## 🎯 Tagline (260 chars max)

> AI transcription that just works. Upload audio or video, get accurate text in 50+ languages — no file size limits, no per-minute billing. Free during beta.

## 📝 Description

Transcribo turns any audio or video file into accurate text in seconds. Built for creators, journalists, podcasters, and professionals who need fast transcription without the complexity.

**What makes Transcribo different:**

- **No file limits** — Upload hours-long recordings. We process them all.
- **Flat credit system** — No confusing per-minute pricing. One credit = one file, any length.
- **50+ languages** — English, Arabic, Chinese, Spanish, French, and dozens more.
- **Multiple export formats** — TXT, SRT subtitles, VTT captions — whatever your workflow needs.
- **Video + audio** — MP3, MP4, WAV, MOV, FLAC, WebM, and 20+ more formats.
- **Privacy-first** — Your files are encrypted in transit and deleted after processing.

Perfect for: podcast show notes, interview transcripts, meeting minutes, video captions, lecture notes, and content repurposing.

## 👋 Maker's First Comment

Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I built Transcribo because I was tired of transcription tools that either cost $20/month or limit you to 10 minutes per file.

My goal was simple: a tool that just works. Drop a file, get text. No confusing pricing tiers, no file size caps, no "upgrade to unlock" dark patterns.

The stack is Next.js + Supabase + Volcengine ASR (one of the best speech recognition engines for multi-language accuracy). We support 50+ languages out of the box — including Arabic, which is surprisingly rare.

**Current status:** Beta — completely free while I iron out the rough edges. I'm shipping improvements daily based on feedback.

## 💡 Tactical Tips

- **Best day to launch:** Tuesday or Wednesday (Monday everyone is catching up, Friday is dead)
- **First hour is critical:** The first 10 upvotes/comments set the trajectory
- **Don't ask for upvotes:** Ask for feedback. Genuine engagement > artificial votes
- **Reply fast:** Under 15 minutes for the first 6-8 hours
- **Tagline is everything:** It's the first (and sometimes only) thing people read
